Overview

SnapCode ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices related to data collection and usage when you visit and use SnapCode.

No Data Collection

We design SnapCode to be privacy-first. By default, SnapCode does not collect, store, or share personal data. Snippets you paste or upload remain on your device and are not sent to our servers unless you explicitly choose to save them to a backend service.

Usage Data & Cookies

SnapCode does not track usage data such as IP addresses, browser fingerprints, or other telemetry. We do not use cookies or third-party trackers in the default client-side-only mode.

Security of Snippets

When operating in client-side mode, snippets are stored in memory or session-local blob URLs and are only accessible in your browser session. If you choose to save snippets to a server (for permanent sharing), please review that service's privacy practices before uploading.

Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., documentation, libraries). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

Children

SnapCode is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them.
