Privacy Policy

Effective Date

SnapCode ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage when you visit our website.

1. No Data Collection

SnapCode is designed to ensure complete anonymity. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data, information, or code you upload or paste on our platform. All interactions with our Site are entirely anonymous.

Usage Data

We do not track or collect any usage data, including IP addresses, browser types, or any other identifiable information.

Cookies

SnapCode does not use cookies or any similar tracking technologies to gather information about your activity on our Site.

2. Security of Your Code

Although we do not store or access the code you upload or paste, we implement security measures to ensure that the code you share via our service remains private and is only accessible through the unique shareable link generated for you.

3. Third-Party Links

The Site may contain links to third-party websites that are not affiliated with us. Once you have used these links to leave our Site, any information you provide to these third parties is not covered by this Privacy Policy.

4. Policy for Children

SnapCode is designed for general use and does not knowingly solicit information from or market to children under the age of 13.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You are encouraged to periodically review this Privacy Policy to stay informed of updates.
